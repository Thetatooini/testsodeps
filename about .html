About SODEPS

SODEPS (Société de Développement et d’Exploitation du Permis du Sud) is a Tunisian oil exploration and production company based in Tataouine. It operates in partnership with ENI (Italy) and ETAP (Tunisia).

Headquartered in Tunis, SODEPS manages several petroleum fields and promotes sustainable energy development in southern Tunisia.
